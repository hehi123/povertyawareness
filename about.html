About us

This website aims to raise awareness on the key issues of poverty to its audience. We all know that poverty is a major problem in society, but it seems that not many people are really aware of how dangerous poverty is. What this website wants to happen is to change our audience's outlook on poverty and make them aware of the truth on poverty.

Meet the authors
