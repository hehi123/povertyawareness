Poverty: Why is it a problem and how to fix it

Be Aware of Poverty and Help us end it!

This website is aimed to help end poverty by using the money we receive as donations to raise charities and provide basic needs for the 1.3 billion people who are considered to be living in "extreme poverty".

This website also aims to make the issue of poverty known to all, and to share to the world that we can do something about this issue. We humans are capable of ending big things like numerous diseases, and we know for sure that we can do the same to poverty.
