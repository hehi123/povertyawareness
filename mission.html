Our Mission

As people who want to do something about ending poverty, we want to raise funds to establish charities across the world to help the 1.3 billion people living in extreme poverty.


________________________________________________________________________________________________


What we plan to do

We plan to raise $1,000,000,000 to support out cause. We will do this through a funding campaign in this website. All of the money donated will be given to raising charities across the world, whose purpose is to end poverty.


________________________________________________________________________________________________


How does the donation work?

In the form, you will be asked for your email, method of donation (through PayPal, Fundly, Qgiv, Mightycause), and how much you are going to donate. Any amount of donation will be appreciated, from as little as $1 to as much as $1000. You may make multiple donations.
