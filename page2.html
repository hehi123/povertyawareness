How you can solve poverty as a person?

Poverty as a whole is difficult to solve, since poverty will always exist as long as people will be greedy. However, if the public and the government work together to cut poverty, then it is possible to eliminate poverty.

Creating more jobs can be a start to cutting poverty. The reason there are people in poverty is because of insufficient jobs. Instead of begging in the streets, an ideal solution is to find a job instead. However, there are not enough jobs to support the whole population. While NCR (National Capital Region) can support a large population because of its cities, there are still people looking for jobs but cannot seem to get one.

Minimum wage also affects poverty. Minimum wage is the lowest wage employers can pay their employees. In 2018, the minimum wage in the Philippines was Php 537.00/day. With the increasing inflation occurring in the country, this may not be enough to support a large family. A family of 3 perhaps, but not a family of 7.

The reason money is the cause of as to why poverty exists is because people need to buy food, healthcare, shelter, and clothes to live. However, programs that can provide them the essential needs can also be a big help in reducing poverty. Programs such as free education can help them. Providing them healthy sanitation and healthcare will also greatly benefit them.

Are there any organizations that support the fight against poverty?

1. Children's Hunger Alliance- This association centers basically around giving nutritious and sound feast alternatives to some low-salary youngsters to strengthen a solid way of life since many don't approach this kind of way of life.

2. Oxfam America- Most popular for taking out treacheries concerning hunger, poverty, and other social issues, Oxfam America gathers the required voices so that there will be genuine change concerning the issue of hunger

3. The Hunger Project- Unlike the first two organizations, The Hunger Project teaches the people instead of directly giving their needs. They show the individuals, who are for the most part are rurals or the individuals who have low pay, fundamental abilities and other continuing practices. They end hunger issues “by empowering people to lead lives of self-reliance, meet their own basic needs and build better futures for their children.”

4. Society of St. Andrew- A purposeful network of individuals committed to serve the ravenous and poor, they have several ways of to feed the community. The Society of St. Andrew not only distributes crops, but also harvests crops such as potatoes. These donations make life better for the people with low income.

5. Mazon- Mazon deals with poverty with 2 main solutions: (1) they support other organizations that also dedicate to helping the hungry (2) They are conducting researches to solve the hidden causes of hunger. Their projects incorporate a support angle to arrive at legislators and vital activities to constantly create thoughts inside the community.

These are just a few of the many, many organizations that help in the fight against poverty. And you can, too.
